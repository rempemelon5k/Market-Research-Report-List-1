<p><p>The magnetic incremental encoder market is segmented into two primary types: linear magnetic encoders and rotary magnetic encoders. Linear magnetic encoders measure position along a straight path, offering precise feedback for applications like automation and CNC machinery. In contrast, rotary magnetic encoders track angular position and rotational speed, making them ideal for applications in robotics, automotive, and industrial machinery. Both types utilize magnetic principles to provide high accuracy and durability, making them essential for various precision tasks in modern technology.</p></p>
