<p><p>The shrinkable polyester film market is divided into three main types: PVC shrinkable film, OPS shrinkable film, and PETG shrinkable film. PVC shrinkable film is favored for its cost-effectiveness and versatility, commonly used in packaging. OPS shrinkable film is known for its excellent clarity and gloss, making it ideal for cosmetic and food packaging. PETG shrinkable film offers superior durability and resistance to impact, making it popular in high-end packaging applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on performance and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
