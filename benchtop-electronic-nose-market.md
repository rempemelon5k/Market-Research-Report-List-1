<p><p>The benchtop electronic nose market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Metal Oxide Type**: Utilizes metal oxide sensors that change conductivity in response to gases.</p><p>2. **Electrochemical Type**: Relies on electrochemical reactions to detect volatile compounds.</p><p>3. **Conductive Polymer Type**: Uses polymers whose conductivity varies with gas exposure, indicating specific odors.</p><p>4. **Photoionizing Type**: Employs ionization of gas molecules using light, providing sensitive detection.</p><p>5. **Others**: Encompasses emerging and hybrid technologies. Each type offers distinct advantages for various applications in odor detection and analysis.</p></p>
