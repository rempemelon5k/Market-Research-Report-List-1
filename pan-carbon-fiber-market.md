<p><strong>The PAN Carbon Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular-Tow Carbon Fiber</li><li>Large-Tow Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PAN carbon fiber market is primarily categorized into regular-tow and large-tow carbon fibers. Regular-tow carbon fibers have smaller filament counts, typically used for high-performance applications requiring superior tensile strength and stiffness, such as aerospace and sports equipment. In contrast, large-tow carbon fibers comprise a greater number of filaments, making them more cost-effective for industries like automotive and construction, where weight reduction and lower production costs are essential. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance and budgetary requirements.</p></p>
