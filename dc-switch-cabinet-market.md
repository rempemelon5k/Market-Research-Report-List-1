<p><p>The DC switch cabinet market comprises two primary types: low voltage switch cabinets and high voltage switch cabinets. Low voltage switch cabinets are designed for applications involving lower voltage levels, typically under 1,000 volts, and are used to manage and distribute electrical power in residential and commercial settings. In contrast, high voltage switch cabinets operate at voltages exceeding 1,000 volts, serving critical roles in industrial, utility, and transmission systems, ensuring reliable power management and safety in high-demand environments.</p></p>
