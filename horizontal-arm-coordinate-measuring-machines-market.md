<p><p>The Horizontal Arm Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMMs) market is divided into two main types: Computer Numerical Control (CNC) and Manually-Controlled systems. CNC CMMs offer automated precision measurement, allowing for higher efficiency and accuracy in quality control processes, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing. In contrast, Manually-Controlled CMMs require operators to position the arm and take measurements physically, providing flexibility and lower initial costs. Each type serves specific applications, balancing the need for precision, cost, and operational complexity.</p></p>
