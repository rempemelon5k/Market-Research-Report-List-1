<p><p>CNC 5-axis machining centers are classified into vertical and horizontal types, each catering to specific machining needs. Vertical 5-axis centers feature a vertical spindle orientation, ideal for large workpieces and complex geometries, offering superior visibility and accessibility. In contrast, horizontal 5-axis centers utilize a horizontal spindle, enabling efficient chip removal and better stability for heavy parts. Both types enhance precision and reduce setup times, playing a crucial role in industries like aerospace, automotive, and medical manufacturing.</p></p>
