<p><p>The Food and Beverage Plastic Packaging market is primarily divided into two types: flexible and rigid packaging. Rigid packaging, on the other hand, consists of bottles, boxes, and containers made from solid plastic, providing robust protection and preserving the integrity of the contents. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing product shelf life and ensuring safe distribution.</p></p>
