<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Scroll Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hermetic Scroll Compressors</li><li>Semi-hermetic Scroll Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electric scroll compressor market comprises two main types: hermetic and semi-hermetic scroll compressors. Hermetic scroll compressors are completely sealed units, integrating the motor and compressor in one casing, making them ideal for space-constrained applications and ensuring minimal leakage. Semi-hermetic scroll compressors, on the other hand, feature a separable motor and compressor design, allowing for easier maintenance and replacement. Both types offer efficiency and reliability, playing a crucial role in vehicle air conditioning and climate control systems.</p></p>
