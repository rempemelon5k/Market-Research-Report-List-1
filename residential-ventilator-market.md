<p><p>The residential ventilator market consists of three main types: Positive Pressure Ventilators, which introduce fresh air into the home, improving indoor air quality; Negative Pressure Ventilators, which expel indoor air to create a vacuum effect, helping to remove pollutants; and Bidirectional Flow Ventilators, which facilitate simultaneous intake of fresh air and expulsion of stale air, promoting balanced ventilation. Each type serves specific needs, enhancing comfort and air quality in residential environments.</p></p>
