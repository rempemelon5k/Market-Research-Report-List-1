<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Mineral Fluid Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffinic Lubricant</li><li>Naphtenic Lubricant</li><li>Aromatic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine mineral fluid lubricants market comprises three primary types: paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants, derived from saturated hydrocarbons, offer excellent thermal stability and low volatility, making them ideal for high-performance engines. Naphthenic lubricants, sourced from cycloalkanes, provide superior solvency and excellent lubrication at various temperatures. Aromatic lubricants, containing unsaturated hydrocarbons, are known for their high viscosity and lubricating properties, typically suited for specialized applications. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing engine efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
