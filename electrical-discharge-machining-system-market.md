<p><p>The Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) system market is primarily divided into two types: Wire-cut EDM and Forming EDM. Wire-cut EDM utilizes a thin, electrically charged wire to cut through metal, allowing for intricate shapes and precise tolerances, making it ideal for complex parts. Forming EDM, on the other hand, employs specially shaped electrodes to create details in the workpiece. Both methods excel in producing high-quality components but cater to different manufacturing needs and design complexities in the industrial sector.</p></p>
