<p><p>The Micro Water Spraying Fertilizer Integrated Machine market encompasses two main types: Film Irrigation and Under Film Irrigation. Film Irrigation involves the application of water and nutrients through a permeable film laid over the soil, enhancing moisture retention and nutrient distribution. Under Film Irrigation, on the other hand, allows for water and fertilizer to be delivered beneath a protective film, promoting efficient root absorption and minimizing evaporation. Both methods improve crop yield and optimize resource usage in agricultural practices.</p></p>
