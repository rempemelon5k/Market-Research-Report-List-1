<p><strong>The Geothermal Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flash System</li><li>Binary Cycle System</li><li>Dry Steam System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geothermal generator market comprises three primary types: Flash System, Binary Cycle System, and Dry Steam System. </p><p>Flash Systems utilize high-pressure geothermal fluids that, when brought to the surface, "flash" into steam, driving turbines. Binary Cycle Systems transfer heat from geothermal fluid to a secondary fluid, which vaporizes and drives a turbine, allowing for lower temperature resources. Dry Steam Systems directly use steam from geothermal wells to turn turbines, representing the oldest method of generation. Each system optimizes energy extraction based on resource characteristics.</p></p>
