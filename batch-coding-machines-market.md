<p><p>The Batch Coding Machines market is divided into two primary types: Contact Coding and Non-Contact Coding. Contact Coding machines operate by making direct contact with the surface of the material, using methods like stamping or inkjet, which ensure clear and durable markings. In contrast, Non-Contact Coding machines apply codes without physical contact, utilizing technologies such as laser or thermal transfer, resulting in higher speeds and reduced wear on equipment. Both types serve varied industrial needs for product identification and traceability.</p></p>
