<p><ul><li>Lubricated Compressor</li><li>Oil-Free Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental compressors market is primarily divided into two types: lubricated compressors and oil-free compressors. Lubricated compressors utilize oil to reduce friction, enhancing performance and longevity but may require more maintenance due to oil management. In contrast, oil-free compressors eliminate the risk of oil contamination, producing cleaner, drier air, making them ideal for sensitive dental applications. Both types cater to different clinic needs, balancing performance, maintenance, and air quality considerations essential for dental practices.</p></p>
