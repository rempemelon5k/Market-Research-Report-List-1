<p><p>The Single Patient Hemodialysis Machine market is categorized into three main types: HD Type, HF Type, and Other. HD Type machines primarily use conventional hemodialysis methods to filter blood, effectively removing toxins and wastes. HF Type machines, or high-flux systems, enhance this process with advanced membranes that allow for better clearance of larger molecules. Other market types may include specialized machines designed for unique patient needs or specific conditions, broadening the accessibility of dialysis therapy.</p></p>
