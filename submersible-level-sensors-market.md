<p><p>Submersible level sensors are crucial for measuring liquid levels in various industries. The market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Gauge Pressure Type**: Measures pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, ideal for open tanks.</p><p>2. **Absolute Pressure Type**: Measures pressure relative to a vacuum, suitable for closed systems.</p><p>3. **Differential Pressure Type**: Measures the difference between two pressure points, effective in complex applications with varying conditions. Each type serves specific needs based on application requirements and environmental factors.</p></p>
