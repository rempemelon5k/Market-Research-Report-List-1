<p><p>The ostomy pouches market comprises three primary applications: colostomy, ileostomy, and urostomy. Colostomy pouches are used to collect waste from the large intestine after a portion has been diverted. Ileostomy pouches serve a similar purpose but are designed for the small intestine. Urostomy pouches collect urine from the urinary system after surgical diversion. These products are essential for individuals with conditions requiring bowel or bladder diversion, enhancing quality of life through comfort, discretion, and skin protection.</p></p>
