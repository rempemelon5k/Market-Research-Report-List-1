<p><p>The radiation monitoring market consists of three primary types: X-ray, gamma ray, and beta ray monitoring. X-ray monitoring focuses on detecting and measuring X-ray radiation, commonly utilized in medical and industrial applications. Gamma ray monitoring involves tracking high-energy radiation, often used in nuclear power facilities and environmental assessments. Beta ray monitoring detects the presence of beta particles, primarily in research and medical contexts. Each type addresses specific safety and regulatory needs, ensuring protection from harmful radiation exposure.</p></p>
