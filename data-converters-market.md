<p><strong>The Data Converters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ADC</li><li>DAC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data converters market consists primarily of two main types: Analog-to-Digital Converters (ADC) and Digital-to-Analog Converters (DAC). ADCs transform analog signals into digital data, enabling processing in digital systems, essential for applications like audio, imaging, and telecommunications. Conversely, DACs convert digital data back into analog signals, crucial for output applications in audio, video, and control systems. Together, these converters facilitate seamless communication between the analog and digital worlds, playing a pivotal role in modern electronic devices.</p></p>
