<p><p>The Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor market includes two primary types: pressure sensors and seat belt tension sensors. Pressure sensors detect the weight and position of occupants in a vehicle seat, enabling the system to differentiate between adults and children, thereby enhancing safety features like airbag deployment. Seat belt tension sensors monitor the force applied by the seat belt on the occupant during braking or collision, providing critical data to optimize restraint systems and improve overall passenger safety.</p></p>
