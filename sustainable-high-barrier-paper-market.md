<p><p>The Sustainable High-Barrier Paper market encompasses two main types: one-sided coated and two-sided coated paper. One-sided coated paper features a coating on only one side, enhancing print quality for graphics while maintaining the other side's natural texture. In contrast, two-sided coated paper is coated on both sides, offering superior strength and printability for a variety of applications. Both types provide high barrier properties, making them ideal for sustainable packaging solutions that protect contents while reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
