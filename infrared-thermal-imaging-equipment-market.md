<p><ul><li>Hot Type</li><li>Quantum Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Thermal Imaging Equipment market can be categorized into two main types: Hot Type and Quantum Type. Hot Type thermal cameras operate based on traditional thermal detection methods, utilizing materials like microbolometers for higher sensitivity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, Quantum Type thermal cameras leverage advanced semiconductor technologies, providing exceptional sensitivity and resolution, ideal for high-precision tasks in scientific and military applications. Both types serve distinct user needs across industries such as security, building inspection, and healthcare.</p></p>
