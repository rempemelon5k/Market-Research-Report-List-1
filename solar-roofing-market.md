<p><p>The solar roofing market includes several types based on energy integration. **On-Grid systems** connect to the public electrical grid, allowing users to send excess energy back. **Off-Grid systems** operate independently, relying on battery storage for energy supply, ideal for remote areas. **Hybrid systems** combine both on-grid and off-grid features, providing flexibility and energy security. Lastly, **Grid-Interactive systems** enable seamless interaction with the grid, optimizing energy use and allowing trade between consumers and providers while enhancing resilience and efficiency.</p></p>
