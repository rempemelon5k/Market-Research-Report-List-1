<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Titanium Nitride (TiN) target market is primarily segmented into two types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, stationary surfaces used in physical vapor deposition processes, offering reliable coating uniformity for various applications. In contrast, rotating targets are designed to spin during deposition, enhancing material utilization and providing more consistent coating properties. Both target types are utilized in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and automotive, where TiN coatings are essential for improving wear resistance and surface hardness.</p></p>
