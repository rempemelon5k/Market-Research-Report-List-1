<p><p>The cellulose-based osmosis membrane market comprises three main types: polysulfone, polypropylene, and polyacrylonitrile. Polysulfone membranes are known for their thermal stability and resistance to oxidation, making them suitable for diverse applications. Polypropylene membranes offer chemical resistance and lightweight properties, ideal for filtration processes. Polyacrylonitrile membranes provide excellent mechanical strength and compatibility with various solvents, enhancing their utility in aquaculture and water treatment. These materials cater to the increasing demand for efficient filtration technologies across multiple industries.</p></p>
