<p><p>RF baluns are crucial components in radio frequency applications, functioning to convert between different types of signal configurations. An unbalanced to balanced balun transforms a single-ended signal into a balanced signal, enhancing noise rejection. A balanced to balanced balun connects two balanced signals, ensuring symmetry and optimal performance. Meanwhile, an unbalanced to unbalanced balun manages signals that are both unbalanced, helping maintain signal integrity and reduce interference. Each type serves to optimize signal quality and efficiency in RF systems.</p></p>
