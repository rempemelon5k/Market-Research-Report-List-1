<p><p>Soft Tag Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) systems include various technologies designed to prevent theft in retail environments. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) systems use unique tags for tracking inventory. Acoustic Magnetic (AM) anti-theft systems employ magnetic fields to detect tags. Radio Frequency (RF) systems work by emitting radio waves to trigger alarms when unauthorized items pass through sensors. Electromagnetic Wave (EM) systems utilize electromagnetic fields for detection, while "Other" encompasses emerging or less common technologies that enhance security measures.</p></p>
