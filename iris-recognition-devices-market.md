<p><p>Iris recognition devices are categorized into two main market types: single eye and double eye systems. Single eye devices capture the iris pattern of one eye, offering a compact and cost-effective solution for identity verification. In contrast, double eye systems scan both eyes, enhancing accuracy and security by leveraging complementary data from both irises. While single eye systems are suitable for various applications, double eye systems are preferred in high-security environments where precision is paramount for authentication.</p></p>
